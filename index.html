Nacho

Nacho Cheese is so loving and full of attitude. Whether she is snuggling at home or out on the street, she definitely has a presence. She loves ear scratches, patrolling enclosed spaces, and high-fives!

Nyx

Nyx is a shadow of mischief and plastic's worst enemy. She was found by a dumpster, so it seems she was born for it. She is very smart and has learned how to sit and high-five by watching Nacho and earning a well-deserved treat.

Orion

Orion is our scaredy cat. He was once the braver of the two siblings, but now he hides from the vacuum. He loves treats and will sometimes steal food from his sister's bowl. He might get in trouble sometimes, but he knows how to behave and is learning to high-five, just like both of his sisters.
